                         ANIMATION DETAILS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Circles (Radius: 28px each):
• Yellow Circle (#92): Starts lower-left, velocity (2.5, -3.0)
• Cyan Circle (#91): Starts lower-right, velocity (-2.0, -2.5)
• Both bounce off frame walls with perfect reflection
• Numbers displayed in black centered on circles

Weapons (Rotating continuously):
• Boomerang: V-shaped with curved arms, yellow, at (200, 180)
• Spear: Shaft + triangular tip, cyan, at (400, 180)
• Both rotate at 0.05 radians per frame (~3° per frame)

Frame Box:
• Position: (120, 140) to (480, 500)
• Size: 360 × 360 pixels
• Style: Black outline, 2px thick, no fill

Text Elements:
• Title: "Boomerang VS Spear" - centered at y=20, black
• Left stat: "Throw Damage: 13" - at (120, 520), yellow
• Right stat: "Damage/Length: 3.5" - right-aligned at (480, 520), cyan

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Drawing APIs Used:
• DrawBox() - background and frame
• DrawCircleAA() - circles (filled + outline)
• DrawLineAA() - weapon components
• DrawTriangleAA() - spear tip
• DrawString() - all text

Physics:
• Simple velocity-based movement
• Axis-aligned bounding box collision with walls
• Velocity reversal on collision

Rendering:
• Double-buffered (DX_SCREEN_BACK)
• ClearDrawScreen() each frame
• ScreenFlip() to present

Performance:
• Text width calculations done once at startup
• Rotation uses sin/cos from standard math library
• Runs smoothly at DxLib's default frame rate
